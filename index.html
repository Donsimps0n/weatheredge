Architecture

Scan → Forecast → Edge → Trade

Three automated steps from market discovery to on-chain execution.

01
Market Discovery
Queries Polymarket Gamma API for all weather-tagged events. Parses question titles to extract city, date and temperature unit. Fetches GFS, ECMWF, UKMO, MeteoFrance forecasts in parallel.
02
Edge Calculation + AI
Builds weighted consensus temperature, calculates dynamic σ based on hours-to-resolution and model spread. Runs Normal CDF probabilities per outcome. Claude AI returns BUY_YES/BUY_NO/SKIP with confidence.
03
Paper or Live Execution
When edge exceeds threshold, Trader executes via FOK market order with 2% slippage fallback. Positions tracked with 5-layer exit monitoring. Full SQLite ledger for P&L.

FAQ

Common questions

What weather models does WeatherEdge use?+
GFS (31-member ensemble), ECMWF IFS025 (51-member ensemble), UKMO, and MeteoFrance. Weighted consensus: ECMWF 35%, GFS 25%, UKMO 20%, MeteoFrance 20%.
How does edge calculation work?+
Builds weighted consensus temperature, calculates dynamic σ based on hours-to-resolution and model spread, runs Normal CDF probabilities per outcome bin, computes edge vs market price, applies quarter-theoretical EV gating capped at 15% bankroll.
What blockchain does it use?+
Polygon blockchain using USDC, connected to Polymarket's Central Limit Order Book (CLOB) via their official API. Private keys never leave your device — all signing happens client-side.
Is the bot running 24/7?+
Yes. WeatherEdge runs on Railway cloud infrastructure continuously, 24/7, even when your computer is off. Scans every 5 minutes, refreshes forecasts every 10 minutes. Telegram alerts on edge discovery.
